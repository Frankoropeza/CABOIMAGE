After photographing hundreds of weddings throughout Los Cabos, I have had the privilege of working in virtually every major venue this extraordinary destination has to offer.

Each one is unique. Each one has its own character, its own light, its own magic. But some consistently create the conditions for wedding photography that takes your breath away.

This is my honest, experience-based guide — written from behind the camera.

## One&Only Palmilla

There is a reason One&Only Palmilla tops virtually every list of Los Cabos wedding venues. The combination of impeccable service, the chapel overlooking the calmer waters of San José Bay, and the lush tropical grounds creates a setting that is simultaneously grand and intimate.

**For photographers:** The chapel light in the late afternoon is extraordinary. The ocean-view terraces provide a canvas that almost photographs itself.

**Best for:** Couples seeking timeless elegance and full-service luxury in a property steeped in history.

## Capella Pedregal

Perched atop a dramatic cliff above the Pacific, Capella Pedregal is one of the most visually striking properties in all of Mexico. Accessible only by a private tunnel through the mountain, it feels like entering a secret world.

**For photographers:** The clifftop ceremony locations offer some of the most dramatic backdrops I have ever worked with. Sunrise and sunset here are simply incomparable.

**Best for:** Couples who want dramatic, cinematic images and a sense of arrival that guests will never forget.

## Esperanza, Auberge Resorts Collection

Esperanza represents the soul of Los Cabos luxury — warm, personal, rooted in the beauty of the natural landscape. The Pacific-facing pools, the casitas nestled into the hillside, the organic garden — everything here has been designed with extraordinary care.

**For photographers:** The natural rock formations and terraced property create endless variety. The golden hour here is among the best I have experienced anywhere in the destination.

**Best for:** Couples who value warmth and authenticity alongside world-class luxury.

## El Dorado Golf & Beach Club

A private residential community with an extraordinary beach club and event facilities, El Dorado offers something rare in Los Cabos: access to a calm, swimmable beach in a setting of manicured beauty.

**For photographers:** The contrast of the emerald golf course against the blue of the ocean, and the wide, accessible beach, create tremendous variety within a single property.

**Best for:** Couples who want a mix of beach and garden settings with a sense of exclusivity.

## Villa del Arco

Set on Medano Beach — the most swimmable beach in Cabo San Lucas — Villa del Arco offers the iconic Arch of Cabo San Lucas as a natural backdrop visible from the property. Few venues can claim one of the world's most recognizable natural landmarks as their backdrop.

**For photographers:** The combination of the beach, the arch in the distance, and the resort facilities offers tremendous visual variety throughout the day.

**Best for:** Couples who want that quintessential Cabo San Lucas experience — and that iconic arch in their photos.

## Hacienda Encantada Resort & Spa

Built in traditional Mexican hacienda style, Hacienda Encantada brings a warm, colorful aesthetic that stands apart from the modern luxury of many Los Cabos properties. The terracotta tones, the arched corridors, the tropical gardens — it is a deeply romantic setting.

**For photographers:** The architecture creates beautiful frames and shade. The colors respond beautifully to the warm Los Cabos light.

**Best for:** Couples who love color, warmth, and a sense of Mexican cultural character in their wedding experience.

## Choosing Your Venue

After years of photographing in all of these spaces, my advice is always the same: visit in person if at all possible, and think about your wedding as a whole experience — not just a single moment.

The best venue is the one that feels most like *you*. And wherever you choose, Los Cabos will provide the light, the landscape, and the energy to make your photographs extraordinary.
