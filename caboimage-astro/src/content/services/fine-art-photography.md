## Photography Beyond the Assignment

Fine art photography is where craft becomes expression. It is the work I create not because a client requested it, but because a moment, a landscape, or a light demanded to be recorded.

## The Light of Baja California

There is a quality of light in Baja California Sur that is unlike anywhere else on earth — the way it falls on desert rock, the way it ignites the ocean at dusk, the way it filters through morning marine layer before burning off to reveal the blue sky above the Sea of Cortez.

This light has shaped my vision as a photographer. It is the raw material of my fine art work.

## For Collectors and Spaces

My fine art photography is available as limited edition prints for private collections, hospitality environments, galleries, and residential interiors. Each print is produced to the highest standards of archival quality.

If you are designing a space that deserves art of genuine depth and local character, I would love to discuss what is possible.
