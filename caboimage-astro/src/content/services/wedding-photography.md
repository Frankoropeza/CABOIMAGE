## Your Los Cabos Wedding Story, Told in Images

Los Cabos is one of the world's most breathtaking wedding destinations — where dramatic desert cliffs meet the Pacific, golden sunsets ignite the horizon over the Sea of Cortez, and the energy of this extraordinary place makes every celebration feel like magic.

I am Miguel Ventura, and for over five years I have had the privilege of documenting love stories in this incredible corner of the world. My approach combines editorial elegance with genuine documentary storytelling — ensuring your photos feel both timeless and deeply personal.

## What I Capture

A wedding day is not a series of posed moments. It is a living, breathing story — one that unfolds in stolen glances, in the trembling of hands during vows, in the eruption of laughter on the dance floor. My job is to be present for all of it, invisible when needed, ready when the moment arrives.

From the quiet intimacy of getting ready to the grand celebration with family and friends, I document every layer of your day with care.

## The Los Cabos Difference

There is nowhere else quite like Los Cabos. The quality of light here — especially during the golden hour — is extraordinary. I know how to harness it, whether you are exchanging vows at a clifftop chapel, on a private beach at Palmilla, or in the lush gardens of a boutique resort.

Years spent living and working in this destination mean I know the hidden spots, the best angles, the timing of the light. That local knowledge shows in every image.

## My Process

Every wedding begins with a conversation. I want to understand your vision, your story, your priorities. Then, on your day, I work seamlessly alongside your coordinator and vendors to ensure nothing is missed — while always staying out of the way of the experience itself.

A photograph should show emotion, feeling, natural expression, and detail that have the power to last forever. That is what I bring to every wedding I photograph.
